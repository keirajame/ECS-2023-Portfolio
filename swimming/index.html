I have swam for around 8 years.

I have swam competitively for almost 6 years.

I swam on a rec league team called the "Penguins" from when I was 6 until I was 7. From then on I swam on a rec league called "Hurricanes" now known as "SeaMonsters" and by the time I was 9 had moved up to their competitive league team named "Legends". I have been swimming with the same coaches now for around 7-8 years.

A swimmer I have always looked up to is Katy Ledecky. Katy Ledecky is a 7 time olympic gold medalist and 19 time World Championship gold medalist. She swims distance freestyle and is known for holding the world record in the 800 and 1500 meter freestyles. She is the most decorated female swimmer in all of history.
